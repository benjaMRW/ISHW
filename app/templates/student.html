Where to Buy

The official Burnside High School uniform can be purchased at:

  • Main Uniform Shop: Located at school campus (Building A, Room 12)
  • Online Store: www.burnsideshop.co.nz
  • Second-hand Uniforms: Available at the PTA shop every Wednesday 3-4pm

Uniform Requirements

All students must wear the complete uniform including:

  • School shirt/blouse with logo
  • School skirt/trousers
  • School jacket (winter)
  • Black leather shoes
